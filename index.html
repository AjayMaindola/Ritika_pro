Arames Lifestyle is your trusted partner in transforming brand visions into reality with expert apparel design and manufacturing solutions. ABOUT US

Based in India, Arames Lifestyle is a leading fashion consulting company- equipped with experts having over a decade of experience in global apparel design and sourcing. We specialize in end-to-end solutions for fashion brands worldwide, blending creative consulting, ethical sourcing, and production and technical expertise to support your brand’s growth. Having worked with top global brands and Indian retail giants, our team is closely aligned with evolving trends, market insights, and consumer demands.

We combine market research, fabric and packaging sourcing, and go-to-market strategies to ensure your brand not only meets today’s standards but also thrives in the fast-paced fashion landscape. We’re deeply adaptive to the ever-changing world of fashion sourcing and the influence of social media, understanding that today’s brands often need smaller batches and seasonally responsive production. With our experienced partners and community, we bring flexibility and insight to help brands launch products quickly and effectively.

Whether you’re an established brand or an emerging clothing company, Arames Lifestyle is here to turn your ideas into reality. From fashion design consultancy to sustainable garment sourcing, we offer tailored services that align with your vision and values, making us the ideal partner for custom, high-quality apparel needs.
